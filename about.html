About Me

Name and Birth

My name is Khalia Dogan. I am 20 years old and I was born in Cleveland, Ohio. I have lived in Ohio my whole life. My birthday is on October 17th.

Interests

I like to draw and make digital art, I also like to create stories with my drawing. I also like to play video games, my personal favorites are Minecraft, Final Fantasy 7, and Left 4 Dead 2. I also like robotics and graphic design, they offer a nice challenge that lets me be creative and apply my skills.

Goals

My goal is to graduate from Kent State University and pursue a career in either game design or graphic design. I would like to have a career where I can pursue my interests and be able to enjoy what I do in a long-term setting. Another goal of mine is to be stable in life financially and career-wise and someday give back to my parents once I secure my future, I want to thank them for their unwavering support throughout my life. Their love and faith in me have given me the strength and determination to succeed.
